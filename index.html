Letters from Elmo are a Dublin-based jazz band founded in 2000.

Since then we've gone on to develop a loyal following around the country and have entertained countless couples on their special days.

Our latest single 'Video Placeholder' is out now.

We are also one of Ireland's most successful wedding bands having been voted Waterford Whispers Wedding Band of the Year in 2019.

Do you have a wedding, corporate event or birthday shindig that just needs some kickin' tunes?

We've got you covered!
