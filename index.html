Letters from Elmo are Ireland's premiere pop-rock events collective.

Formed in Dubin back in 2007, L.F.E have come a long way from gigging around the city's dingy dive bars.

Fast forward to 2020 and we have mastered every genre, have toured worldwide and have supported epic acts such as Busted Chifarobe, Logan's Sun and Klondike Kittens.

Weddings? Corporate Events? Parties? We play them all!
